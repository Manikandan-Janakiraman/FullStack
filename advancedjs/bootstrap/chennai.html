Safety Concerns — Delivery & Logistics

Identify common hazards for delivery personnel: road safety risks, fatigue, lifting injuries and adverse weather exposure. Prioritize training and PPE to reduce incidents.

  • Road & traffic accidents — safe driving training & fatigue management
  • Manual handling — correct lifting techniques & trolleys
  • Personal security — secure routes, partner check-ins
  • Weather exposure — appropriate clothing & rest breaks
